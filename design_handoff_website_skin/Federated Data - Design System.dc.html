How the pool works

Every contributor keeps a node they control. Nodes federate — they exchange signals about the work, never raw ownership of it — so the collection grows richer without anyone surrendering their rights. The community sets the terms of use together, and any member can see how their data travels, reshape those terms, or withdraw entirely.

Why federation

Centralised archives ask you to trust an owner. A federated one asks only that you trust the agreement — and gives you the tools to check it. That difference is the whole project.
